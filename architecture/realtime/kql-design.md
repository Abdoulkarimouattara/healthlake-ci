# KQL Design

## Overview

Kusto Query Language (KQL) is used to analyze streaming healthcare events stored in Eventhouse.

Its purpose is to transform raw telemetry into operational intelligence that supports rapid decision-making.

---

# Analytical Scenarios

KQL queries are designed to identify situations requiring immediate attention, including:

* Abnormal heart rate.
* Low oxygen saturation.
* High body temperature.
* Device connectivity failures.
* Critical patient alerts.

---

# Operational Intelligence

KQL enables healthcare teams to:

* Filter live events.
* Aggregate telemetry.
* Detect abnormal patterns.
* Produce real-time KPIs.
* Feed operational dashboards.

---

# Example Outcomes

Typical operational insights include:

* Number of critical patients.
* Active medical devices.
* Alerts by hospital.
* Alerts by department.
* Recent abnormal vital sign readings.

These insights are immediately available to healthcare professionals through real-time dashboards.

---

# Design Principle

Transform continuous healthcare telemetry into actionable operational intelligence through fast, scalable event analytics.
