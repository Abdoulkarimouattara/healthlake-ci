# Eventstream Design

## Overview

Eventstream serves as the entry point for all real-time healthcare telemetry generated by connected medical devices.

Its primary responsibility is to continuously ingest streaming events and deliver them to downstream analytical services with minimal latency.

---

# Streaming Sources

The platform receives telemetry from connected patient monitoring devices, including:

* Heart Rate
* Oxygen Saturation (SpO₂)
* Body Temperature
* Blood Pressure
* Respiratory Rate

Each measurement is transmitted as an independent event and immediately ingested into Microsoft Fabric.

---

# Eventstream Responsibilities

The Eventstream layer is responsible for:

* Continuous event ingestion.
* High-throughput event processing.
* Reliable event delivery.
* Real-time data routing.

Rather than storing historical information, Eventstream focuses exclusively on moving streaming data through the platform efficiently.

---

# Downstream Integration

Captured events are forwarded to Eventhouse, where they become available for real-time querying, operational monitoring, and alert detection.

---

# Design Principle

Eventstream acts as the ingestion gateway of the real-time architecture, ensuring that operational telemetry reaches analytical workloads with minimal delay.
