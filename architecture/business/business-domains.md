# Business Domains

## Overview

HealthLake CI is organized into several business domains representing the core operations of a modern healthcare system.

Each domain owns a specific set of business entities and processes while contributing to a unified healthcare analytics platform.

---

# 1. Healthcare Organization

This domain manages the organizational structure of the healthcare network.

### Business Entities

* Region
* Hospital
* Department
* Room
* Bed
* Medical Equipment
* Staff

---

# 2. Patient Care

This domain manages the complete patient journey.

### Business Entities

* Patient
* Admission
* Diagnosis
* Treatment
* Prescription
* Discharge

---

# 3. Patient Monitoring

This domain captures real-time patient telemetry generated by connected medical devices.

### Business Entities

* Medical Device
* Telemetry
* Vital Sign
* Clinical Alert

---

# 4. Emergency Operations

This domain manages emergency department activities.

### Business Entities

* Emergency Visit
* Triage
* Doctor Assignment
* Waiting Time

---

# 5. Enterprise Analytics

This domain provides trusted business KPIs for operational and strategic reporting.

### Business Areas

* Hospital Performance
* Patient Flow
* Bed Occupancy
* Equipment Utilization
* Clinical Quality Indicators

---

## Business Domain Relationships

The healthcare domains are interconnected to support a complete view of hospital operations while maintaining clear business responsibilities.
