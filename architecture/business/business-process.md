# Business Process

## Overview

HealthLake CI models the complete patient lifecycle from hospital admission to discharge while integrating operational and real-time monitoring processes.

---

# Patient Journey

```text
Patient Registration
        │
        ▼
Emergency / Scheduled Admission
        │
        ▼
Doctor Assessment
        │
        ▼
Diagnosis
        │
        ▼
Treatment
        │
        ▼
Room Assignment
        │
        ▼
Vital Sign Monitoring
        │
        ▼
Discharge
```

---

# Supporting Processes

Alongside patient care, the platform continuously supports:

* Hospital resource management
* Bed occupancy tracking
* Medical equipment monitoring
* Clinical alert management
* Operational reporting

---

# Business Outcome

The platform provides a unified operational view of patient care, hospital resources, and clinical monitoring while enabling trusted analytics across the healthcare network.
