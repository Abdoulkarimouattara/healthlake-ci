# User Personas

## Overview

HealthLake CI is designed to support multiple user groups across the healthcare ecosystem.

Each persona has different analytical needs and access permissions.

---

# Executive Leadership

## Ministry of Health

### Responsibilities

* Monitor national healthcare performance
* Compare regional KPIs
* Support strategic planning

---

## Regional Health Directorate

### Responsibilities

* Monitor regional hospitals
* Analyze regional healthcare performance
* Coordinate operational improvements

---

# Hospital Operations

## Hospital Director

### Responsibilities

* Monitor hospital activity
* Track resource utilization
* Improve operational efficiency

---

## Department Manager

### Responsibilities

* Supervise department performance
* Monitor bed occupancy
* Track patient flow

---

# Clinical Staff

## Doctor

### Responsibilities

* Access patient information
* Monitor assigned patients
* Review clinical alerts

---

## Nurse

### Responsibilities

* Monitor patient vital signs
* Track medical devices
* Respond to operational alerts

---

# Security Principle

Every persona accesses only the information required for their responsibilities through role-based security and governed data access.
